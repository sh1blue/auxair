Our AC Services in Ernakulam, Aluva, North Paravur & Kalamassery

AC Installation & Dismantling

Planning to install a new split AC or window AC at your home or office in Ernakulam, Aluva, or Kalamassery? Our certified technicians handle precision setup and safe dismantling of all premium AC units — with proper bracket fitting, copper piping, and electrical connection. We also handle full relocation when you're shifting homes or offices across North Paravur, Cherai, and surrounding areas. All major brands supported: LG, Samsung, Daikin, Voltas, Hitachi, and more.

Jet Wash & Deep Cleaning

Kerala's high humidity causes dust, mold, and fungal buildup on AC coils and filters very quickly — reducing cooling efficiency and increasing electricity bills. Our advanced jet wash and deep cleaning service uses high-pressure washing equipment to thoroughly clean indoor and outdoor units at your home or office in Ernakulam, Cherai, or Kalamassery. Result: optimized airflow, better indoor air quality, and a noticeably cooler room. Recommended every 3 months in Kerala's climate.

Gas Charging & Leak Fixing

If your AC is running but not giving cold air, low refrigerant gas or a gas leak is the most common cause — especially in older units across Kerala homes and offices. Our technicians visit your location in Ernakulam, North Paravur, Aluva, or Kalamassery, perform rigorous diagnostics to identify leaks, seal them professionally, and recharge the correct refrigerant (R22 or R32) to the right pressure level. Safe, precise, and affordable gas charging service at your doorstep.

All Mechanical Repairs

Is your AC making unusual noises, tripping frequently, or simply not turning on? Our expert technicians are trained in comprehensive troubleshooting and repair of all AC mechanical components — including compressors, fan motors, control boards (PCBs), capacitors, and thermostats. We visit homes, flats, shops, and offices across Ernakulam, Cherai, Aluva, and Kalamassery for fast, reliable repairs with durable results. Same-day AC repair service available across all our service areas.

Periodic Maintenance Packages

The best way to avoid sudden AC breakdowns — especially during Kerala's scorching summer and humid monsoon — is a scheduled maintenance plan. Our periodic maintenance packages include comprehensive inspections of all AC components, filter cleaning, coil washing, drain pipe clearing, gas pressure checks, and a full system health report. Available for homes, apartments, offices, and commercial spaces across Ernakulam, North Paravur, Aluva, Cherai, and Kalamassery. Keep your AC running smoothly all year round.

Duct Cleaning & Air Quality

Commercial buildings, offices, hospitals, and hotels in Ernakulam and Kalamassery that use central AC systems need regular duct cleaning to maintain healthy indoor air quality. Kerala's damp climate causes dust, allergens, and bacteria to accumulate inside ducts — leading to musty smells, poor airflow, and health issues. Auxcare's professional duct cleaning team visits your premises and thoroughly cleans the entire ductwork, improving air circulation for everyone inside the building.
